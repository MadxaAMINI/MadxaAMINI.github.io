Young Eagles Academy

Established in 2002, Young Eagles Academy has always been a symbol of passion, teamwork, and perseverance. Our journey from a local club to competing in top leagues showcases the hard work and dedication of every player, coach, and supporter. With multiple championships under our belt, we strive not just to win, but to inspire the next generation of soccer talent.

"More than a team, a family with a purpose. Together we rise, together we conquer."

Our Commitment to Excellence

Aims

Achieve Competitive Success

Our aim is to consistently compete at the highest level of football, striving for league titles and cup victories every season.

Facilitate Affordable Transfers

To facilitate transfers of soccer players from one team to another on an agreed purchase amount and to eradicate poverty within the community .

Raise Awareness

To offer awareness in drug and substance use To avoid early marriages and school dropouts

Develop World-Class Talent

We invest in youth development programs, nurturing future stars of the game to ensure our long-term success on the pitch.

Vision

Our vision is to build a club that is respected both locally and globally, known for its integrity, excellence, and its contribution to the sport.

We aspire to lead both on and off the pitch, setting standards in football management, community engagement, and sustainability.

Philosophy

Attacking Football

We believe in playing an exciting, attacking brand of football that excites the fans and embodies the spirit of the game.

Community-Driven

Football is more than just a game; it’s a community. Our philosophy is rooted in giving back and engaging with our supporters.
